How to Cook Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 slices Bacon,cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 8 1/2 cups of water
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 1/4 cups finely grated cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tsp each salt and pepper
  • Chopped fresh Parsley

Instructions:

  1. Add Bacon and 1/2 cup of waterto a large non-stick skillet and bringto a simmer over medium-high heat
  2. Allow to simmer until water evaporatesabout 6-7 miniutes
  3. pour into a medium mixing bowl then add 1 Tbsp rendered baconfat
  4. Mean while bring 8 cups of water to boil in a large ducth oven
  5. Slowly pour and whisk 1/2 cup Pasta water into egg mixture, then slowly pour mixture over pasta while tossing to coat.
  6. Let pasta rest,tossing frequently , 2-4 miniutes until sause has thickened slightly and coats pasta.
